    PHISHING INDICATORS

    📧

    Sender Mismatch

    Display name says "PayPal" but the actual email domain is @paypa1-support.com — subtle character swaps are common.

    🔗

    Suspicious Links

    Hover before clicking. URLs using subdomains like paypal.evil.com or IP addresses are almost always malicious.

    ⏰

    Urgency & Threats

    "Your account will be closed in 24 hours!" — artificial urgency is designed to bypass your rational thinking.

    🖊️

    Grammar & Spelling

    Legitimate organisations proofread. Strange phrasing, odd capitalisation, and spelling mistakes are warning signs.

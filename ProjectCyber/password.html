STRONG PASSWORD PRINCIPLES

🎲

Use a Passphrase

4 random words (e.g. "purple-cloud-river-lamp") are both strong and memorable. Length beats complexity.

🔑

Password Manager

Use Bitwarden, 1Password, or similar to generate and store unique passwords for every site.

🚫

Never Reuse Passwords

One breach exposes all your accounts. Even changing one character from your main password is not enough.

📱

Enable 2FA

Two-factor authentication stops attackers even if they have your password. Use an authenticator app, not SMS.
